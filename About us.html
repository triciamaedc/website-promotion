ABOUT US

Nature of Our Business

    Plàtano Fresa is a delectable dessert. It's all about producing and selling
    bananas coated with strawberries. The Plànato Fresa are creating their own product by creating
    unique taste and unique to students' eyes because no one else is doing this yet, and it's also not available
    in other markets. The primary goal of this small business is to achieve at least an 80% return on investment upon implementation.

Mission

    Our mission is to build a business that is worth building that lasts long.
    Also to achieve this we will work at our best and communicate with each co-worker so we can strive for our success.
    We want to build this business so we can provide a better quality of food that they deserve in an affordable way.
    We are dedicated to help our local farmers and pay them enough to get what they want.

Vision

    Our goal is to create better food and to inspire many people
    throughout the world and realize what our potential can do. For our work,
    we want to expand our knowledge to create a new food and experiment a lot so we can find an
    iconic one like our latest product called "Plátano Fresa".
