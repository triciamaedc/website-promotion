Plátano Fresa

Ingredients

  • 1kg Banana (saba)
  • 10 pcs strawberry
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 evaporated milk
  • 6 pcs Hansel Crackers (for breadcrumbs)

Directions

  1. *In a small bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  2. *In another bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream.
  3. *Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  4. *Place the strawberries in a bowl and pour the honey syrup over them. Toss to combine.
  5. *Roll each banana half in the flour mixture, then dip it in the egg wash.
  6. *Place the rolled bananas, skin side down, on the prepared baking sheets.
  7. *Spoon the strawberries over the bananas. Sprinkle the reserved crumbled unsalted butter over the top.
  8. *Bake the fruit layer for 30 minutes, then reduce the oven temperature to 325°F (160°C) and bake for an additional 1 hour, flipping the bananas halfway through the baking time.
  9. *Let the bananas cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
